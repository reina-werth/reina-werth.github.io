Algorithm

Our algorithm is made on Teachable Machine, and has been trained at 500 epochs on images of dice taken by Reina Werth and Carrie Fassbinder (see our training images here). We took the images at multiple different angles and on several different faces to help the algorithm identify the differences between the dice types based mostly on shape. We knew we would face particular issues with distinguishing D10s and Percentile Dice, as they are the same shape, and only differ in the numbers on the face. On testing, the algorithm is about 89% accurate, with particularly high accuracy for D20s (95%) and particularly low accuracy for D10s (77%). We believe these two accuracy rates can be explained by the sheer number of images of D20s we were able to train the algorithm on, because D20s have the greatest number of faces of our dataset, and confusion for percentile dice in the case of the D10s.
