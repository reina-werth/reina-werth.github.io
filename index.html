Welcome!

Welcome to Project 3: Machine Learning, where we teach AI to categorize various pictures of dice. This project aims to explore AI ethics, biases in algorithmic systems, and the societal implications of artificial intelligence from Joy Buolamwini's book, Unmasking AI.

This site covers the project's statement of purpose, lessons learned from Unmasking AI, and an overview of how algorithms are classified. Each section is designed to provide visitors with a comprehensive understanding of these crucial topics.

We invite you to navigate through the various pages to learn more about the intersection of technology, equity, and ethical AI development.
